# Environment
1. Open a browser window for the project repository.
    * This will be used for viewing the instructions.
1. Open a second browser window for the project repository.
    * This will be used for doing your work.
1. In the second browser window:
    1. Click the **Projects** tab.
        * Appears at the top of the window.
    1. Click on the name of the project (e.g. `ITS DevSecOps Adventure`)
    * Select the view with the name that matches your role (tabs at the top of the screen).
    * Make labels visible
        1. Click on the down arrow next to the name of the view (e.g. `Development`).
        1. Select **Fields** under **Configuration**
        1. Select **Labels** from the **Hidden Fields**
