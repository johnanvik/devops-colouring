# Sprint Activities
## Starting Place
1. Go to the `Testing` tab of the project board.

## Sprint Planning - Team
1. As a development team, select a product request from the `Market` colomn
1. Move the product request to the `Backlog` column to claim it.
1. Add the team's label the issue.
   1. Click on the name of the product request.
   1. Click on **Labels** on the righthand side.
   1. Select the label with your team name (e.g. `Team 1`).
1. Read the `Definition of Done` document to know the requirements of a completed drawing.

## Sprint Execution
1. Moniter the `Testing` coloumn for completed issues from your team.
    - Use the labels to know which issues to test.
1. Confirm that completed drawings meets the `Defintion of Done` criteria.
1. If the drawing is complete:
    1. Set the **Status** to **Release**
       - Dropdown under `Projects` on the lefthand side.
1. If the drawing needs more work:
    1. Create a comment describing the problem.
    1. Set the **Status** to **Development**

# Sprint Review
Discuss with your team the following three questions:
1. What are the top two things that went well?
1. What are the top two things that did not go so well?
1. What are two changes your team would like to make in the next sprint?
